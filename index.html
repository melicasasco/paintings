Gustavo

Acrylic on canvas.

About

Born in Buenos Aires, my passion for painting and drawing has been with me as long as I can remember. From a very young age, I excelled in school subjects related to the arts, both visual and musical, and attended workshops from an early age. At the age of 15, I studied with who would become my lifelong mentor, Cristina Santander, a renowned visual artist from Argentina. While studying in her workshop, I participated in many group exhibitions and had a solo exhibition at my university called ''Silent Images".

I graduated in Art History and Cultural Management and worked in art galleries and antique houses. In recent years, I've been working as a designer in agencies, and at present, I am working as a frontend developer for a non-profit organization while providing design support in a design agency.

My eyes have always been drawn to lights and shadows, in search of a new world, a parallel universe in which nothing is as we know it.
