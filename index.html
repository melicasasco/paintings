Paintings

Gustavo

Acrylic on canvas.

This is Gustavo Cerati's portrait. He is one of my favorite Argentine musicians. During my adolescence, I was very influenced by his lyrics and music. He passed away some years ago. I painted him in a world full of dreams and enchantment, where I imagine he is now.

Noelio

Oil on canvas.

Noelio, my best friend, my first cat, my entire world. We used to go to the countryside and spend hours outside. This tree was one of his favorites.

My cats in front of Santa Felicitas Dome

Acrylic on canvas.

This painting depicts the dome of Santa Felicitas Church in the Barracas neighborhood of Buenos Aires. The place holds a mythical legend and a love story. It is especially meaningful to me because my grandparents used to live in front of this magical building.
